What is "To The Moon"?

Investors who hope for a big increase in their cryptocurrency or other asset’s value will use the phrase “to the moon.” Since the moon sits high in the sky, the term is a play on other terms such as “sky-high valuation,” “soaring prices,” and the like.

Why we use goto?

The goto statement can be used to alter the flow of control in a program. Although the goto statement can be used to create loops with finite repetition times, use of other loop structures such as for, while, and do while is recommended. The use of the gotostatement requires a label to be defined in the program.
